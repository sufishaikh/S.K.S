The Ant and The Grasshoper 
 In a field one summer's day a Grasshoper was 

      hopping about, chirping and singing to its heart's 

      content. An Ant passed by, bearing along with great efforts 

      an ear of corn he was taking to the nest. "why not come and chat 

      with me", said the Grasshopper ,"instead of toiling and moiling 

      away? "I am helping to lay up food for the winter ," said the ant and
      recommend to you to do the same 

      "why bother about winter? said the Grasshopper, "we havve got plenty of
      food at present
